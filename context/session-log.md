- Uses `SDL_LoadWAV` to load the sound file, `SDL_BuildAudioCVT` / `SDL_ConvertAudio` to convert to device format (22020 Hz, AUDIO_S16SYS, stereo), then applies volume + pan scaling and queues PCM via `SDL_QueueAudio`
